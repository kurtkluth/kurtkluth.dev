# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 18, 2026**

This site is built to need as little of your information as possible. The
short version: there are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no
tracking, and the games save their data on your own device rather than
sending it anywhere. The rest of this page explains the details.

## Who runs this site

kurtkluth.dev is run by me, Kurt Kluth. If you have any question about
privacy, email [kurt@kurtkluth.dev](mailto:kurt@kurtkluth.dev).

## What I collect

**Information you give me.** The only way you actively send me anything is by
emailing me. If you do, I receive your email address and whatever you choose
to write. I use it to reply and to follow up on the conversation, and nothing
else.

**Information collected automatically by the hosting.** The site is served as
static files through GitHub Pages, fronted by Cloudflare. Like essentially
all web hosting, those providers process basic technical data needed to
deliver pages and to protect the site, such as your IP address and browser
user-agent, in their own server logs. That processing is theirs, under their
policies; I do not add my own analytics or logging on top of it, and I do not
build profiles of visitors.

**Information stored on your device.** The games keep your scores, settings,
and progress in your browser's local storage, and the site remembers small
preferences such as your light or dark theme choice the same way. This data
lives in your browser, on your device. It is not transmitted to me, and I
cannot see it. You can clear it at any time through your browser's settings.

The site's search is handled entirely in your browser with a search index
that ships with the site; your searches are not sent anywhere.

## How I use information

I use the little I receive only to:

- reply to and manage correspondence you start;
- keep the site running, secure, and working as intended;
- comply with the law where I am required to.

I do not sell your information, and I do not share it for advertising. There
is nothing to sell, because I do not collect it for that purpose.

## Who else is involved

The only third parties in the picture are the infrastructure providers that
make the site work: GitHub (hosting) and Cloudflare (content delivery and
security), plus my email provider if you write to me. They act as service
providers and handle data under their own privacy policies. I would also
disclose information if the law genuinely required it.

## Cookies and tracking

I do not use tracking or advertising cookies, and there is no third-party
analytics on the site. The only client-side storage is the local storage
described above, used for game saves and your theme preference. It is
functional, stays on your device, and is not used to follow you around the
web.

## Data retention

Email correspondence I keep for as long as it is useful for the conversation
and any follow-up, then I delete it. The technical logs kept by the hosting
providers are retained and rotated under their own schedules, not mine.

## Your choices and rights

Because I hold so little, your controls are simple:

- **Your device data** is yours to clear whenever you like, through your
  browser.
- **Your email correspondence** you can ask me to show you or delete; email
  [kurt@kurtkluth.dev](mailto:kurt@kurtkluth.dev) and I will help.

Depending on where you live, laws such as the GDPR or California's privacy
laws may give you additional rights over personal data. I am happy to honor
reasonable requests under them; just get in touch.

## Children

This site is not directed at children under 13, and I do not knowingly
collect information from them.

## Where processing happens

The site is hosted in the United States and served globally through a content
delivery network. If you visit from elsewhere, the technical data involved in
delivering the site may be processed in the United States.

## Changes to this policy

If I change how any of this works, I will update this page and the "Last
updated" date above.

## Contact

Privacy questions go to [kurt@kurtkluth.dev](mailto:kurt@kurtkluth.dev).

Lisa Climber is an original browser-based arcade climbing platformer, drawn
in pixel art and built around one glorious problem. Going up. Load it and
the title screen introduces itself as **Summit Smash**. Lisa Climber is the
project's name here in the Kluth Studios collection; Summit Smash is the
name the game wears on the mountain. Same game, two hats.

You can play it right now, for free, at
[lisaclimber.kluthstudios.com](https://lisaclimber.kluthstudios.com). No
install, no account, keyboard or touch.

## The objective

Climb every layer of every stage without running out of hearts.

That is the entire contract. Each stage is a vertical tower of platform
layers: you start at the bottom, the way out is at the top, and everything
in between (dropping icicles, patrolling critters, slick ice, and a red
kill floor glowering below) is negotiable as long as you keep moving and
protect your hearts.

## The mountain

The climb is organized into **six stages**, each built from **eight
layers** of platforms. The first stage is **Frostpeak**, an icy opener
that teaches you early to respect frozen footing. Each stage brings its
own look and its own trouble as you work through the set.

The HUD keeps you oriented the whole way up: which stage you are on, which
layer you have reached, how many hearts remain, and what your score and
multiplier are doing. [How to Play](./how-to-play.md) walks through all of
it.

## What a run feels like

- **Platforms with personality.** Brick, ice, water-topped, and grass
  surfaces, each with its own feel underfoot.
- **Hazards on a schedule.** Icicles that break loose and drop, and
  critters that patrol their platforms.
- **Pickups worth a detour.** Gems and money bags that raise your score
  and feed your multiplier.
- **A floor you never want to meet.** The red kill floor waiting at the
  bottom of the screen.

The full systems tour lives in [Gameplay](./gameplay.md), and
[Tips](./tips.md) will save you a few hearts on the way up.

## Free, in your browser

Like every Kluth Studios game, Lisa Climber is free to play with no
install and no account. It runs in any current browser (Chrome, Edge,
Firefox, or Safari) on desktop or mobile, with both keyboard and touch
support. The attract screen says it plainly: press any key, or tap, to
play.

## Part of Kluth Studios

Kluth Studios is the banner for Kurt Kluth's browser games and
experiments, and the games are made for Lisa. That is how a game called
Summit Smash ends up with Lisa's name on the project. You can see where it
sits in the collection on the
[Lisa Climber project page](/projects/lisa-climber).

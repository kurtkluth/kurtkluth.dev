## 2026-07-18: Joined the collection

**Status: Active**

Lisa Climber joined the Kluth Studios collection on kurtkluth.dev.

What arrived with this entry:

- The live game, playing under its in-game title **Summit Smash**, at
  [lisaclimber.kluthstudios.com](https://lisaclimber.kluthstudios.com),
  free in the browser with keyboard and touch support.
- A full documentation set for players:
  - [Overview](./overview.md) explains what the game is and how the mountain is built.
  - [How to Play](./how-to-play.md) covers launching the game and reading the HUD.
  - [Gameplay](./gameplay.md) details platforms, hazards, collectibles, and hearts.
  - [Tips](./tips.md) offers practical advice for spending fewer hearts.
  - [FAQ](./faq.md) has quick answers, including the two-names question.
  - [Changelog](./changelog.md) is this page, ready for whatever comes next.
- A home on the portfolio: the
  [Lisa Climber project page](/projects/lisa-climber).

## What "Active" means

Active is the healthiest status a project here can have: the game is live,
playable today, and part of the ongoing collection. If that ever changes,
or when the game grows, this page is where it gets written down.

Future updates to the game or its documentation will be recorded here as

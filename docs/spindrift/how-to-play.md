Load [spindrift.kluthstudios.com](https://spindrift.kluthstudios.com), press
`Enter`, and you are flying. This page is everything standing between you and a
respectable first run.

## Starting a game

Press `Enter` on the title screen. You spawn as a lone ship in the middle of a
rock field. That is the entire onboarding; the rest is physics and reflexes.

## The objective

- **Clear the rocks.** Shoot them; they split into smaller, faster pieces before
  they finally vanish.
- **Handle the saucers.** They cross the field and shoot at you. Dodge their
  shots and take them down; the small ones are worth serious points.
- **Survive as long as possible.** Everything on screen is trying to occupy the
  same space as your ship.
- **Chase the high score.** Your best run persists as the HI score; the only
  boss in this game is your previous self.

## Controls

| Key | What it does |
|---|---|
| `←` / `→` | Rotate the ship left / right |
| `↑` | Thrust: accelerate in the direction the nose points |
| `Space` | Fire |
| `Shift` | Hyperspace: emergency teleport to a random spot (risky) |
| `P` | Pause |
| `M` | Mute |
| `G` | Toggle the glow effect |
| `F` | Fullscreen |

Two of those deserve a closer look:

- **Thrust** adds speed; nothing takes it away except thrusting the other
  direction. There are no brakes; the ship keeps drifting. This is the game's
  real difficulty and its whole charm; [Gameplay](./gameplay.md) covers the
  physics.
- **Hyperspace** yanks you out of certain death and drops you somewhere random,
  possibly somewhere worse. A last resort, not a habit.

## Touch controls

On phones and tablets, touch is supported: on-screen controls cover rotating,
thrusting, and firing, so the full game is playable with two thumbs. Fullscreen is
worth it at any screen size.

:::tip

For your first run: tap `↑` in short bursts, keep the ship barely drifting, and
pick off the big rocks from a distance. Speed kills, usually you.

:::

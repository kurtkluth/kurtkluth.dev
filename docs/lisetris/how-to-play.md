# How to Play

## Start a game

1. Open [lisetris.kluthstudios.com](https://lisetris.kluthstudios.com) in a
   modern browser.
2. On desktop, play with the keyboard; on mobile, use the on-screen touch
   controls.

## The loop

- **Move and rotate** each falling piece into position.
- **Complete horizontal lines** to clear them and score.
- **Clear multiple lines at once** for bigger scores.
- The game **speeds up** as you progress — survive as long as you can.

## Tips

- Keep the stack flat; tall, jagged towers create holes you'll pay for later.
- Don't hoard a perfect spot for one specific piece — play the piece you
  have, not the piece you want.
- When the speed ramps up, prioritize survivable placements over perfect ones.

:::tip
The in-game prompts are the authoritative controls reference — check them on
first launch.
:::

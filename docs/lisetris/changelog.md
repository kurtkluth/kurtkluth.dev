## 2026-07-18: Joined the collection

**Status: Active**

Lisetris joined the Kluth Studios collection on kurtkluth.dev.

What arrived with this entry:

- The live game at
  [lisetris.kluthstudios.com](https://lisetris.kluthstudios.com), a
  romantic neon falling-block puzzle game, made for Lisa, free in the
  browser with keyboard and touch support.
- A full documentation set for players:
  - [Overview](./overview.md) covers the game, the dedication, and the modes.
  - [How to Play](./how-to-play.md) covers the goal and the full control table.
  - [Gameplay](./gameplay.md) explains Classic and Relaxed in depth, plus scoring.
  - [Tips](./tips.md) offers practical stacking advice.
  - [FAQ](./faq.md) has quick answers, including who Lisa is.
  - [Changelog](./changelog.md) is this page, ready for whatever comes next.
- A home on the portfolio: the
  [Lisetris project page](/projects/lisetris).

## What "Active" means

Active is the healthiest status a project here can have: the game is
live, playable today, and part of the ongoing collection. When Lisetris
grows, or if anything about its status changes, this page is where it
gets written down.

Future updates to the game or its documentation will be recorded here as

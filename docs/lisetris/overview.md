# Lisetris

**Lisetris** is Kluth Studios' take on the classic falling-block puzzle:
rotate and place pieces, clear lines, and hang on as the pace climbs.

**Play it now:** [lisetris.kluthstudios.com](https://lisetris.kluthstudios.com)

## The objective

Pieces fall; you place them. Complete horizontal lines to clear them and
score. The stack rises, the speed increases, and the run ends when the board
fills. Simple rules, endless depth.

## Why it's fun

- The falling-block formula is one of the most replayable loops ever designed.
- Speed ramps keep every run tense in its final minute.
- Loads instantly in the browser — perfect for a five-minute break that
  becomes twenty.

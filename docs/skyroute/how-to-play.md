# How to Play

1. Open [skyroute.kluthstudios.com](https://skyroute.kluthstudios.com) in a
   modern browser.
2. Follow the on-screen introduction — Skyroute explains itself as you go.
3. Explore. Experimental projects reward poking at things.

:::tip
Skyroute is an evolving experiment: what you see may differ from what these
pages describe, and the in-experience prompts always win. When something
notable changes, it lands in the [changelog](/docs/skyroute/changelog).
:::

## If you get stuck

- Reload the page — experiments are built to restart cleanly.
- Check [Troubleshooting](/docs/skyroute/troubleshooting) for browser issues.

## 2026-07-18: Skyroute joins the collection

**Status: Experimental.**

Skyroute joined the Kluth Studios collection on kurtkluth.dev. The live game at
[skyroute.kluthstudios.com](https://skyroute.kluthstudios.com) and this
documentation set went up together.

Snapshot of the game as documented today:

- **Two aircraft.** The SL-9 Skylark piston trainer (forgiving, cruise 110 kt)
  and the SwiftJet 100 regional twin-jet (fast and slippery, cruise 250 kt,
  reverse thrust on the runway).
- **Four regions**, each an airport pair flyable in either direction: Verdant
  Coast (MER ⇄ SKY), Amber Mesa (DST ⇄ MVM), Granite Range (ALP ⇄ SUM), and
  Azure Atoll (CKY ⇄ LGI).
- **Eight weather settings.** Clear, Scattered, Cloudy, Overcast, Foggy, Rain,
  Stormy, and Crosswind, plus Day, Sunset, and Night time of day.
- **Four realism levels.** Arcade, Assisted, Realistic, and Custom, plus a
  High detail / Fast graphics switch.
- **An autopilot suite.** Altitude hold and route following via `P`, cycling
  SPD / HDG / ALT / V/S targets with `Tab`, a heading bug, NAV re-arm, and an
  ILS-style APR approach mode.
- **A logbook** of completed flights, saved in your browser.
- **Landing scoring** that rewards touching down on the numbers with a descent
  rate under about 300 fpm.

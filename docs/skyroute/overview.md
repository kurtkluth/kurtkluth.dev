# Skyroute

**Skyroute** is a flight-and-navigation themed interactive experience from
Kluth Studios. It's part of the experimental side of the collection — a
playable idea that keeps evolving.

**Try it now:** [skyroute.kluthstudios.com](https://skyroute.kluthstudios.com)

## What it is

Skyroute is built around taking to the sky: plotting a route and seeing where
it takes you. As an **Experimental** project, its shape changes more often
than the games do — part of the point is discovering what it is right now.

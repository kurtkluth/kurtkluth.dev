**SkyRoute Infinite** is an original open-world browser flight simulator built under
the Kluth Studios banner, and the tagline is the whole pitch: open-world flight,
straight from your browser. No downloads, no DLC, just wings.

Fly it right now at [skyroute.kluthstudios.com](https://skyroute.kluthstudios.com).
It is free, with no install and no account. For the project story, see the
[Skyroute project page](/projects/skyroute).

:::note

Skyroute is **experimental**. It flies, it lands, and it logs your flights, but it
is an active experiment. Expect it to keep evolving between visits.

:::

## What you actually do

Every session starts on the flight setup screen and ends, ideally, on a runway.
You choose:

- **An aircraft.** Either the forgiving SL-9 Skylark piston trainer or the fast,
  slippery SwiftJet 100 regional twin-jet.
- **A region.** Four of them, from green coastline to serious mountains to island
  strips barely long enough for the brave.
- **A route.** Each region is an airport pair, and you pick which direction to fly.
- **Weather.** Eight options, from Clear all the way up to Stormy and Crosswind.
- **Time of day.** Day, Sunset, or Night.
- **A realism level.** Arcade, Assisted, Realistic, or Custom, so the same route
  can be a joyride or a checkride.

Then you take off, fly the route to the destination airport, and land as gently as
you can manage. The smoother the touchdown, the better the score.

## The logbook

Every flight you complete goes into your logbook, kept right in your browser. It is
the quiet motivator of the whole game: once a route is in the book, the next
question is always "fine, but can I fly it at night, in a crosswind, on Realistic?"

## What you need

- A current browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari).
- **A keyboard, strongly recommended.** The game is playable on mobile, but flying
  is a two-hands-on-the-keys experience: pitch and roll on the arrow keys, throttle
  on `W` and `S`, and a row of system keys for flaps, gear, and autopilot.

There is no download and nothing to install. It is a browser game in the fullest
sense. Close the tab mid-flight and the only casualty is your landing score.
